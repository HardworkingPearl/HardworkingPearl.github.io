About Me

I am a fourth-year PhD student in the machine learning group under the Computer Science Department of the University of Western Ontario. I am supervised by Prof. Prof. Charles Ling (Fellow, Canadian Academy of Engineering) and Boyu Wang. Now I am open to post-doctoral and research scientist positions!

Research Interests

I focus on effcient transformer architectures, and the problem of transfer learning with temporal distribution shifts.
